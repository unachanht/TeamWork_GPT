What is TeamWork GPT?

TeamWork GPT is an AI-powered teamwork assistant designed for ENTR550 students. It helps you navigate common team challenges using proven frameworks and templates:

  • Team Charter: Define mission, roles, goals, and norms
  • Meeting Agendas: Plan productive, time-boxed meetings
  • Conflict Resolution: A 6-step framework for working through disagreements
  • Accountability Tracking: Keep tasks, owners, and deadlines visible
  • Peer Feedback (SBI): Give honest, constructive feedback using Situation-Behavior-Impact

Choose a team role to personalize the advice, then pick a situation to get template-structured guidance tailored to your specific challenge.
